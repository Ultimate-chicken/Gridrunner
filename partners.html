Developing cutting-edge tools for the banking industry

Our partnership with AIB allows us to complement our advanced cybersecurity solutions with real-time insights. This allows us to maintain the pace with developments in the industry and to create tailred security measures in accordance with specific needs. A closely-knit partnership as such ensures that financial institutions stay ahead of the curve when it comes to data integrity and confidentiality, taking a proactive approach.

RIT Program

The Reduced Identity Theft Program (RIT) is a collaboration agreeement between Gridrunner and AIB. AIB provides behavioural data for machine learning within the AegisAI environment, which is in turn used to actively monitor identity theft threats to the bank's clients.

CRI Initiative

The Cyber-resilience Initiative (CRI) employs innovative algorithms to ensure automatic recovery of key systems and minimise system downtime. Solutions to critical processes are then integrated into our main platform in order to other financial industry clients more resilient.

Improving the resilience of healthcare

We take pride in collaborating with crucial sectors such as healthcare in ensuring sensitive data is both integral and confidential. Our ethical hacking partnership with the Bon Secours hospital has resulted in an improved awareness of the healthcare data architecture and the patching of several zero-day exploits within the system.

WB Program

The Weekly Bounty (WB) Program has incentivised our red teamers to find vulnerabilities within the system infraestructure through the white-box and black-box modalities. Since the start of the program, 7 Grade-9 vulnerabilities have been patched successfully.

PA Program

The Phishing Awareness (PA) Program is currently ongoing and works by urging hospital employees to notify potentially fraudulent and malicious emails that they have received. These email structurs are not only added to our database, but they are also used to used for machine learning purposes to improve our AegisAI heuristics algorithms.

At the forefront of cyberwarfare

Our partneship with the National Cyber Security Centre ensures that our platform adapts to changes in the global environment, including cyberwarfare and hybrid warfare practices by oversea organisations or institutions. This close connection allows us to respond appropiately to innovative forms of cyberattacks.

ECR Program

The Emergency Capacity Requirement (ECR) Program is one of our most active partnerships so far. It consists of an agreement with the NCSC that in the case of oversea-originated DDos attack, our distributed server network is to be provided to ensure business continuity and service availability.

SR Initiative

The Scam Raiders (SR) Initiative ensures cross-functional communication between the NCSC and ouselves to better protect clients and citizens against malicious attempts. We provided the NCSC entries of our phishing and spam database, and they contact us for joint anti-criminal ventures.

For a more trust-worthy E-commerce

We have partnered with Tesco to ensure that the electronic stores regain public trust and do not give leeway to malicious practices. Particularly, we are collaborating with Tesco in the area of fraud protection and Web Security, which have become increasingly demanding in recent times.

FHA Platform

We have implemented Fraud Heuristics Algorithms (FHA) throughout Tesco's online payment and ordering infraestructure. Special emphasis is placed on return and refund scams that have increased in value and complexity over the years. This data is in turn fed into our machine learning models to ensure constant upgrading.
